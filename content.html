Nepal and it's Eight Wonders

Author:Sundar Gyawali

04 March | 10 min read

Nepal is a beautiful country, blessed with nature’s choicest attractions. The country also boasts of remarkable historical and cultural attractionslike temples and monuments that speak volumes about the rich history and culture of the place. The scenic beauty adds a perfect backdrop to these popular tourist places in Nepal and the climate makes it favorable for the travelers to visit and explore. Though over the years, this country has emerged as a holiday destination, there is still a major part of Nepal that remains untouched by urbanization and the pollution it brings along.
